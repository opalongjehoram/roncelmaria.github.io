Bakhawan

Nestled in the coastal town of Cordova in Cebu, Philippines, lies Bakhawan Ecopark, a breathtaking mangrove forest that has captured the hearts of nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. Bakhawan is a haven of ecological wonder, offering visitors a unique and enchanting experience.

Environmental Education and Community Engagement

Bakhawan Ecopark takes pride in its commitment to environmental education and community engagement. The park offers guided tours and educational programs that highlight the importance of mangroves and raise awareness about conservation. Visitors can also participate in tree-planting activities, contributing to the ongoing reforestation efforts.




Mangrove Forest

Cordova, a small town in the province of Cebu, Philippines, is not just known for its beautiful beaches but also for its stunning mangrove forest. This hidden gem is a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and eco-tourists. Mangrove forests are incredibly important ecosystems that serve as a habitat for various plants and animals. They act as natural filters, protecting coastal areas from erosion and offering a buffer against strong waves and storms.

Visiting the mangrove forest in Cordova is more than just a recreational activity; it is an opportunity to appreciate and learn about the wonders of nature. Walking along the boardwalk and immersing yourself in the tranquil surroundings will leave you with a sense of awe and appreciation for the beauty and ecological importance of mangrove forests. A visit to Cordova's mangrove forest is a must for anyone interested in ecotourism and sustainable travel.



Cordova Reef

Cordova Reef is a beautiful destination located in the province of Cebu, Philippines. This hidden gem offers a serene and picturesque environment perfect for nature lovers and beach enthusiasts. Cordova Reef is situated in the town of Cordova, which is located in the eastern part of Cebu. It is easily accessible via land transportation

The main attraction of Cordova Reef is its stunning beach. The white sand and crystal-clear waters provide a perfect backdrop for relaxation and leisure activities. Visitors can enjoy sunbathing, swimming, snorkeling, and even beach volleyball.




Cordova's Plaza

Cordova, a bustling municipality in the province of Cebu, Philippines, is not only known for its stunning beaches and residential communities but also for its lively public spaces. At the heart of Cordova lies its vibrant plaza, a central gathering place that showcases the town's cultural richness and community spirit

Cordova's plaza offers a glimpse into the heart and soul of Cordova. So, if you find yourself in this charming town, make sure to visit the plaza and experience its lively atmosphere and rich cultural tapestry
