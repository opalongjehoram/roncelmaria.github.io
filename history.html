San Roque Parish Church

Deep in heart of Cordova, Cebu lies a beautiful and historic landmark that has withstood the test of time - the San Roque Parish Church. This magnificent church holds a special place in the hearts of the locals and serves as a testament to the town's rich cultural heritage. Join us as we take a virtual journey to explore the history and significance of San Roque Parish Church.

Spiritual Significance

San Roque Parish Church holds immense religious importance to the people of Cordova. It serves as a sanctuary where the faithful gather for prayer, worship, and religious events. The church is dedicated to Saint Roque, a patron saint of the sick and protector against epidemics. Many locals attribute their healing and protection to the intercession of Saint Roque, further cementing the spiritual significance of this sacred place.
