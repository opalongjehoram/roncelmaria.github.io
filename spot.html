Solea

Located on the beautiful island of Cebu in the Philippines, Cordova is a rising tourist destination known for its pristine beaches and vibrant culture. Among the many attractions that Cordova has to offer, Solea Resort and Spa stands out as a true gem. This luxurious resort is a paradise in itself, offering a complete package of relaxation, adventure, and culinary delights.

Exciting Activities and Amenities

Solea in Cordova offers a wide range of activities and amenities that cater to individuals and families alike. Adventure enthusiasts can indulge in thrilling water sports such as jet skiing, parasailing, and kayaking. The resort also has a swimming pool with a water slide for those who prefer a more relaxed aquatic experience.

Lantaw

If you're a food lover looking for a unique dining experience in Cordova, Cebu, then Lantaw Floating Native Restaurant is a must-visit destination. Nestled amid the pristine waters of Cordova, Lantaw offers both a scenic view and lip-smacking food to satisfy your cravings.

One of the highlights of Lantaw is its unique floating concept. As you step onto the restaurant, you'll instantly feel the gentle rocking of the bamboo floor beneath your feet. The entire dining area is built on wooden stilts, giving you a feeling of being above water.

Visiting Lantaw is not just about the food; it's a complete package. You can take a leisurely stroll along the boardwalk and soak in the stunning views of the sea and the neighboring islands. The staff is welcoming and friendly, ensuring an excellent customer service experience.

Cordova Home Village

Cordova Home Village is a haven for those seeking a quieter lifestyle. The village is designed with careful attention to detail, featuring tree-lined streets, well-manicured gardens, and a sense of community that is hard to find elsewhere. Residents can unwind and relax in the scenic neighborhood, away from the noise and chaos of the city..

Cordova Home Village is more than just a residential community; it is a gateway to a more peaceful and balanced way of living. With its serene surroundings, modern homes, and range of amenities, the village offers an ideal haven for individuals and families looking to escape the fast-paced city life. Discover the serenity of Cordova Home Village and make it your tranquil retreat.

Parola
